About Us

Our History

Started in 2020, with the mindset to facilitate the women's lives in Singapore's society, we create this platform from scratch.

CVShopping, a shopping platform exclusively for women, collects the orders from users and forward them to large e-commerce platform and retailers such as Shopee, Lazada, etc. so that female users could enjoy advantagous prices.
